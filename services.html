Industrial & Process Valves

We provide a comprehensive range of industrial valves for various applications including oil & gas, petrochemical, water treatment, and more. Our valves meet international standards and specifications.

Our Valve Products Include:

  • Gate, Globe, Check, Butterfly, Foot, Safety, Strainer, Bellow Sealed Valves
  • Sizes from DN15 to DN600 (PN 16 to PN 250)
  • Body materials: GG 25, GGG 40, GSC 25 & Stainless Steel
  • Forged Steel Valves (Class 800# to 4500# Size ¼" - 2")
  • API Approved Ball Valves (API 6D & 607)
  • Trunnion & Floating Ball Valves (Fire Safe)

Road Marking Solutions

FlowCube manufactures and supplies a full line of durable thermoplastic products in both alkyd and hydrocarbon formulations engineered to meet BS EN standards.

Our Road Marking Services:

  • Thermoplastic pavement marking material (100% solid, environmentally safe)
  • MMA Rumble Markings for lane delineation
  • Roadline paint (Reflective & Non-Reflective types)
  • Complete road marking application services

Key Benefits:

Superior Performance

Abrasion resistant to maintain glass beads for high retro readings

Visibility

Brighter dry and wet reflections at night

Durability

Extended service life with maintained retroreflectivity

Quick Application

Fast drying in < 5 minutes at temperatures as low as 30°C

Application Process:

1

Surface Preparation

Clean road surface from debris and contaminants

2

Marking Preparation

Chalk markings and string guides for accurate application

3

Material Preparation

Heating to molten state (max 220°C) with continuous stirring

4

Application

Laying with proper width and thickness according to specifications
